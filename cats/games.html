The Liminal Scope

Game Release - February 21st, 2022

Platform: PC - GameJolt

Download Game

The Liminal Scope is a first-person exploration game focused primarily on exploring a catalogue of procedurally generated liminal spaces.

Step into a dimension of your choice, far away from our plane of reality and experience a place full repeating patterns and familiar places, oddly familiar but deeply dreadful.

With the humming buzz of the halogen bulbs lighting your path, as your only companion, the only thing left is to push forward to seemingly no avail.

No end, no goal, no hope.

Dragon Duel

Game Release - May 29th, 2020

Platform: PC - GameJolt, Itch.Io

Download Game

Dragon Duel is a passion project, inspired by the good old releases of Atari 2600.
The gameplay featured is simplistic and reminiscent of the Atari 2600 games and your goal is to destroy the opposing dragon by hitting it 3 times with the arrow!

This is a 2 player title.

This software is open source and VB2019 project can be downloaded here along with all of the assets used.

Dragon Duel Screenshot Gameplay Screenshot

This project represents something special to me, because its the very first project I went 100% trough with, even though I have coded for many years now.

Dragon Duel features partial USB controller support. Please make sure that the controllers are plugged in before starting the game.

This project uses SDL2 libraries and they are self-contained in the project that can be downloaded from the GameJolt or Itch.Io page.
