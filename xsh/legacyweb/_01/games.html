DRAGON DUEL
May 30th, 2020


Dragon Duel is a passion project, inspired by the good old releases of Atari 2600. The gameplay featured is simplistic and reminiscent of the Atari 2600 games and your goal is to destroy the opposing dragon by hitting it 3 times with the arrow!
This is a 2 player title.
