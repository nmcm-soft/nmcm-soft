The Liminal Scope v0.1 Released!

February 21st, 2022 - NDA

Language/Engine: C++/SDL 2.0



Liminal Scope is a first-person exploration game focused primarily on exploring a catalogue of procedurally generated liminal spaces.



Step into a dimension of your choice, far away from our plane of reality and experience a place full repeating patterns and familiar places, oddly familiar but deeply dreadful.

With the humming buzz of the halogen bulbs lighting your path, as your only companion, the only thing left is to push forward to seemingly no avail.

No end, no goal, no hope.



Get lost in a maze of bizzare and procedurally generated architectural anomalies, acompanied by nothing and nobody... as far as you know.



Liminal Scope is being actively developed. Currently, on it's release date 20/02/2022, Liminal Scope contains a single fully complete map, the lost halls, utilizing the developed procedural generation algorithm.
Further maps are planned and are already being developed.

Maps currently in development are based on:
  • Subway stations
  • Hotel walkways
  • Indoor pools
  • Hospitals
  • Malls and foodcourts
